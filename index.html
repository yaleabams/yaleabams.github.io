Experiences

CDO CAMPUS

USTP ( University of Science and Technology of Southern Philippines ), or mostly known as what used to be Don Mariano, is a state university here in Cagayan De Oro, a prestige school that specializes in mostly computer mediated courses. To us first year students, this is a school of new oppurtunities, other than its excellent academics, the college experience including the extra-co seals the deal.


ICT BUILDING

Since the school, specializes in tech, you can expect that facilities like Computer Laboratories are complete and well cared for. Most of our time here in school takes place in the ICT Building or Building 9, most of the learning that prepares us as future IT happens here. Even waiting for the next class, we wait by the spiral stairs almost everyday. Here we meet other fellow students and teachers that are also "techies".


DRAWING BUILDING

Drawing building also known as building 13. We have our purposive communication class here every Tuesday and Friday 4:30pm - 6:00pm in the evening. This is also the place where we always get stuck when there is flood. When it's raining very hard, most of us are already worried and afraid especially when we didn't brought slippers. Nevertheless, experiencing flood is also one of the most memorable and fun experience in USTP especially when you are with your friends.


Pathway

Main Pathway, we met random people exchange smiles multiple times yet we don't know who they are. Isn't it awkward? Many things happened in this pathway specifically at the Flag Pole Pathway this is where we signed our Attendance dor the First time as a Student in USTP. In the same area it is where the Rave Party held at, some programs during intramurals was also held here.You can not enjoy intramuralsif you did not seat at the Gutter of the road in the pathway.We also got to experience passing at the pathway with a knee level of flooded water. Students pass by at the main pathway,so always expect that it is really noisy.And also at the main pathway you can find every announcement or program the school have. So far it was really nice to experience such things.


IT Pathway

IT Pathway or the Technology Street, is the way to Bldg.9 or the ICT Building. And also it is the way where we walk through if we're going to IT Building or our Department Offices. As you can see on the photo it is also where cars and vehicles passes through. on the Left side is the Bldg.9 and on the right side is the Bldg.5 , it is where we buy on the Vendo Machine. We use to stand by here when we are going home and discussing things or talking noisily, or even shouting and laughing together as we go home we bring happy momments with us that happened to our Day.


Black Market

Black market is one of the mysterious place in USTP considering that only few people know this place and where it is located. As you can see in the photo above, black market has only 1 store and its located across the creek, you only need to shout and they will deliver it through a rope and a basket. The first time we heard the word "Black Market" we felt excited and curious at the same time. Also, the way they describe the place and how they transport the foods or things we bought made us more curious what it looks like. Black Market is also one of the things that made USTP unique.
